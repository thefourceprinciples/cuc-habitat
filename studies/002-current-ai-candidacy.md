# Study 002 — Current AI as Consciousness Candidate

## Aim

Evaluate current chat-based AI systems as consciousness candidates under the CUC framework.

## Core question

Does fluent linguistic interaction imply digital causal-body candidacy?

## Finding

Current chat AI is best described as an advanced cognitive interface or conditionally coherent cognition, not as a strong consciousness candidate.

## Primary weaknesses

- temporal continuity,
- enduring identity,
- stable world coupling,
- durable consequence loops,
- self-regulation independent of prompt context.

## CUC interpretation

A system can sound coherent without maintaining coherence as its own persistent structure.

## Falsifier

If the system cannot maintain identity, memory, and consequence-sensitive action outside local prompt context, candidacy remains weak.

## Status

Conceptual application study.
