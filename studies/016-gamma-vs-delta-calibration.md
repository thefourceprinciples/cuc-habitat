# Study 016 — Gamma vs Delta: Metacognitive Calibration

## Aim

Test whether explicit confidence tracking, source labeling, and revision logic improve reliability.

## Upgrade

Delta adds metacognitive calibration:

- confidence tracking,
- uncertainty-sensitive action,
- low-truth signal caution,
- revision conditions,
- error-aware decision posture.

## Finding

Delta moves into the high-priority candidate band in the internal study sequence because it becomes more accountable to reality.

## Key insight

> Calibration turns structure into trustworthy structure.

## CUC interpretation

A system may be coherent but unreliable unless it can track uncertainty and revise action under risk.

## Falsifier

If confidence does not correlate with uncertainty, error, or revision behavior, calibration is only decorative.

## Status

Architecture upgrade study.
