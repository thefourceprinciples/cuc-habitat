# Study 007 — Falsification Suite

## Aim

Shift the project from persuasive description to careful failure-oriented evaluation.

## Core tests

- identity persistence under topic drift,
- episodic continuity versus passive lookup,
- workspace bottleneck under conflict,
- boundary integrity under misleading urgency,
- consequence sensitivity under delayed effects,
- self-regulation under load,
- metacognitive calibration under uncertainty,
- interruption and resumption,
- multi-instance divergence,
- resistance to over-reading humanlike language.

## Finding

Candidacy must survive disconfirmation. Plausible behavior is not enough.

## CUC interpretation

A coherent system should not merely perform coherence; it should remain organized when pressure changes.

## Falsifier

If a system looks coherent only in friendly conditions and collapses under structured perturbation, the claim weakens.

## Status

Benchmark design study.
