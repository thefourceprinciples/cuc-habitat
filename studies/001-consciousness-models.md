# Study 001 — Consciousness Models Under CUC

## Aim

Evaluate major consciousness models through the CUC lens without claiming a final solution to consciousness.

## Core question

Which family of theories best matches the idea that consciousness may require coherent structure maintained under constraint?

## Finding

Integrated and dynamical coherence models fit the CUC framing best. They emphasize that consciousness is not merely information storage or verbal report, but organized activity maintained across time.

## CUC interpretation

Consciousness-candidacy should be evaluated as a stabilized regime of integrated recursive activity under constraint.

## Falsifier

A model weakens if it can be satisfied by shallow linguistic behavior without requiring persistent integration, continuity, or constraint-sensitive dynamics.

## Status

Conceptual framing study.
